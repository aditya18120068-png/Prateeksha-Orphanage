Our Story

“A Shelter of Love, A Future of Hope”

“An orphanage is a place that provides care, shelter, and education to children who have lost their parents or have no one to look after them. It serves as a home where these children find love, safety, and guidance. Beyond meeting their basic needs, an orphanage also helps them build confidence, learn valuable life skills, and grow into responsible individuals. It is not just a shelter, but a family that nurtures hope and creates opportunities for a brighter future.”

Our Mission

“The mission of an orphanage is to provide a safe, loving, and supportive environment for children who have lost parental care. It works to fulfill their basic needs of food, shelter, and education while also focusing on their emotional well-being.”
